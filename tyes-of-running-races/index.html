Tyes of Running Races

5K
5 kilometres, or around 3 miles
The 5K is a beginner-friendly race that is well-liked option for charity events and community runs, providing a reasonable challenge for runners of all skill levels. It is a beginner-friendly race.

10K
10 kilometers, or roughly 6 miles.
For runners who are in between the approachability of a 5K and the endurance needs of lengthier events, the 10K provides a baseline.

Half Marathon
13.1 miles or 21.1 kilometers.
At 13.1 miles, the half marathon is a big step for runners looking to push their endurance; it requires focused training and a deliberate pace.

Full Marathon
42.2 kilometers or 26.2 miles
The full marathon is considered the ultimate in long-distance running, is a significant accomplishment for runners worldwide, and requires a great deal of physical and mental preparation.
